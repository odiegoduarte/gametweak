#### Comandos básicos

~~~sh
cm (Abilita GODMOD)
~~~

~~~sh
dm (Abilita modo debug)
~~~

#### FPS Boot

~~~sh
gfx pp enable 0
~~~

~~~sh
gfx af enable 0
~~~

~~~sh
gfx dt enable 0
~~~

### Desabilitar
- Vsync
- Desfoque de Movimento (Montion Vlur)
- Qualidade do Reflexo (Reflection Quality)

### Desmarcar
- Sun Shalft
- DOF
- Shadows
- SSAO

### Opcional
- View Distance 10
- Field of View 70
- LOD Distance 50%
